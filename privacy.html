Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 30, 2026

TERRION Games ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application "Love Marble".

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information to provide better game services and improve user experience:

  • User Provided Information: Nicknames, anniversary dates, and couple data entered voluntarily within the app.
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, language settings, and IP address for troubleshooting.
  • Usage Data: Game progress, button clicks, round completion rates, and session duration.
  • Transaction Data: When you make a purchase (e.g., Premium Pack), we collect transaction details such as product ID, price, and currency to analyze sales performance. Note: We do not store your credit card information; payments are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the "Love Marble" game service.
  • To calculate D-days and manage couple records locally.
  • To analyze game performance, fix bugs, and improve stability.
  • To measure marketing efficiency: We analyze purchase data (ROAS) to understand the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns using third-party tools.

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information used to identify your device. These tools help us understand usage patterns and improve our app:

  • Unity Analytics: For analyzing game usage and player behavior.
  • Google Analytics for Firebase: For analyzing user demographics, retention, and purchase conversions.
  • Google Ads: For tracking ad conversions and marketing effectiveness.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. We may share generic aggregated demographic information regarding visitors and users with our business partners and advertisers for the purposes outlined above.

5. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.
