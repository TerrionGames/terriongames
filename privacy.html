Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 30, 2026

TERRION Games is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application "Love Marble".

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information to provide better game services and improve user experience:

  • User Provided Information: Nicknames, anniversary dates, and couple data entered voluntarily within the app.
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, language settings, and IP address for troubleshooting.
  • Usage Data: Game progress, button clicks, round completion rates, and session duration.
  • Transaction Data: When you make a purchase, we collect transaction details (product ID, price) to analyze sales. We do not store credit card info.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the "Love Marble" game service.
  • To calculate D-days and manage couple records locally.
  • To analyze game performance, fix bugs, and improve stability.
  • To measure marketing efficiency (ROAS) and ad campaign effectiveness.

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information used to identify your device:

  • Unity Analytics: For analyzing game usage and player behavior.
  • Google Analytics for Firebase: For analyzing demographics and retention.
  • Google Ads: For tracking ad conversions and marketing effectiveness.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information. We may share generic aggregated information with partners for the purposes outlined above.

5. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.
